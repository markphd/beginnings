CHINESE STYLE STIR FRIED RICE



Here's how to make the dish

  1. Place cooked rice in pan (i use dark rice as its healthier but white rice is cool too)
  2. Place some sesame oil, garlic, when hot throw in cooked rice
  3. Fry, chop some carrots and leeks (at least a palm full of each and throw in too)
  4. Beat 2 eggs, make a well and throw into pan too
  5. Add about a fourth cup soy sauce and a tablespoon of honey and a tablespoon of vinegar
  6. Cook rice another 15 minutes
  7. Serve in little bowls and garnish with some left over carrots and leeks!

    8. Enjoy!!!! ( :

    Try with chopsticks for an asian touch!

Simple fresh and delicious! Colors fantabulous too!
From my kitchen to yours with love, Mwahhh! -S

Ingredients:

  • 5 to 6 cups cooked rice
  • sesame oil
  • garlic
  • carrots and leeks
  • 2 eggs
  • soy sauce
  • honey
  • vinegar
