TABULEH SALAD



Here's how to make the dish

  1. start with 2 cups chopped parsely and another 2 cups chopped lettuce,
  2. add half a cup chopped tomatoes, and another of white onions, about a palm full of pre soaked couscous
  3. then in small bowl beat virgin olive oil and calamansi about half a cup each.. salt pepper..
  4. pour over mixed greens and toss!!!
  5. voila.. refreshing tabouleh great with lamb tajine or cumin barbeques!
Simple fresh and delicious! Colors fantabulous too!
From my kitchen to yours with love, Mwahhh! -S

Ingredients:

  • virgin olive oil
  • calamansi
  • salt pepper
  • 2 cups chopped parsely
  • 2 cups chopped lettuce
  • half a cup chopped tomatoes
  • white onions
