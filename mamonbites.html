MAMON BITES WITH STRAWBERRY AND CHOCOLATE SAUCE



Here's how to make the dish

  1. In a large saucpan, melt butter over low heat. To that, add rhum and stir constantly for 3- 4 minutes to cook off alcohol.
  2. Add chocolate pudding.
  3. On a large flat serving plate, place your mamon bites and create a mound.
  4. Pour chocolate sauce over mamon and top with fresh strawberries.
  5. Enjoy!
Simple fresh and delicious! Colors fantabulous too!
From my kitchen to yours with love, Mwahhh! -S

Ingredients:

  • 6- 8 pcs of Mamon, torn into bite-size pieces
  • 1 cup of fresh, ripe strawberries, halved
  • 1/4 cup of chocolate pudding
  • 5 tbsp. of rhum
  • 1 tbsp. butter
