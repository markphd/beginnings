Sabrina's Kitchen is a show that was created, produced and hosted by one of Manila's most passionate food and entertaining enthusiasts - Sabrina Artadi.

It is a 30-minute show that features Sabrina's gastronomic creations- easy, inexpensive gourmet dishes! Dishes that have been inspired from her years of travel around the globe, using locally-available ingredients.

Sabrina's Kitchen first aired over Lifestyle Network on August 3, 2011.
