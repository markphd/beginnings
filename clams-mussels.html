CLAMS & MUSSELS IN SALSA VERDE SAUCE



Here's how to make the dish

  1. Do ahead of time: soak shells for 15 minutes in clean tap water to let them spit out impurities.
  2. Remove beards (hairy tip on the center of the shells) using a scissor.
  3. In a pot of boiling, salted water cook mussels and clams for 10 minutes. Drain.
  4. ANY SHELL THAT REMAINS SHUT AFTER BOILING MUST BE THROWN AWAY!
  5. In a blender, combine parsley, garlic, onions, olive oil, white wine, salt and pepper. Blend to the consistency of your choice- smooth and fine or creamy and chunky.
Simple fresh and delicious! Colors fantabulous too!
From my kitchen to yours with love, Mwahhh! -S

Ingredients:

  • 1 kl. Mussels, soaked in clean water for 15 minutes
  • 1 kl. Clams, soaked in clean water for 15 minutes
  • 2 c. Parsely, chopped
  • 1/4 c. Garlic, chopped
  • 1/2 c. Olive oil
  • Â½ c. White wine or beer
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
