MOROCCAN LAMB TAJINE



Here's how to make the dish

  1. Start with a lamb leg or rib slab on baking dish place slab of lamb
  2. Make small cuts near bone area especially and stuff with peeled garlic cloves
  3. Mix half a cup soy sauce, red wine, yogurt, a fourth cup honey, and a teaspoon each of McCormick cinnamon, cumin, paprika and turmeric
  4. Sprinkle all over lamb
  5. In separate bowl place a cup of couscous in warm water. Let stand.
  6. Then in a hot pan.. saut√©, a palm full of raisins, dried fruits(apricots,apples ,cranberries etc) and cashews
  7. Mix in the couscous now already absorbed in liquid.. add a cup of tomato sauce
  8. When all are mixed in, layer over a large baking dish or pan
  9. Place lamb on top of couscous, pouring all the yummy juices and spices over lamb
  10. Place strips of cut carrots ,eggplant and cabbage on top of lamb
  11. Add a spirnkle of olive oil, yoghurt,drizzle of honey and yoghurt on top
  12. Sprinkle with remaining raisins and nuts
  13. Slice an orange skin and all and garnish over lamb with some rosemarie
  14. Squeeze half a cup of pineapple over entire lamb for guaranteed soft meat!
  15. Cover in foil and cook in pre heated oven for 1 hour and 45 minutes

    16. Enjoy!!!! ( :

Simple fresh and delicious! Colors fantabulous too!
