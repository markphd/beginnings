KOREAN BULGOGI BARBE ALA SABRINA



Here's how to make the dish

  1. In large dish, place boneless chicken cut in strips, fish dory , pork or beef sukiyaki or tenderloin strips
  2. Let each ingredient have its own space on the dish.
  3. Don't mix
  4. Add sliced carrots in strips, onions in rings, eggplant in strips, zucchini, tomatoes.

    5. NOTE: Almost any veg will do!

  5. In large glass, mix equal parts of sesame oil, vinegar, soy sauce, and honey.

    6. OPTIONAL: If you like spice add some chili sauce too, add a palm full of finely chopped garlic.

  6. Mix all together and pour over all the ingredients.
  7. In heated grill pan start to cook.

    8. TIP: This cooks quickly, so I suggest guests kids get involved and cook for themselves.

    I promise there will be no left overs! Best served with my korean fried rice!!! ( :

    This dish is so verstile you can use almost any available ingredient, squid prawns, etc!!!

Simple fresh and delicious! Colors fantabulous too!
From my kitchen to yours with love, Mwahhh! -S

Ingredients:

  • boneless chicken cut in strips
  • carrots
  • onion in rings
  • eggplant in strips
  • zucchini
  • tomatoes
