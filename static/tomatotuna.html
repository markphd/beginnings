SABRINA'S TOMATO TUNA



Here's how to make the dish

  1. In a wide salad dish layer lettuce, corn, peas, artichokes tuna and tomatoes.
  2. Season with salt and freshly cracked black pepper to taste.
  3. For dressing, whisk together equal parts of olive oil and vinegar.
  4. Right before serving, pour vinaigrette on top of the salad.
Simple fresh and delicious! Colors fantabulous too!
From my kitchen to yours with love, Mwahhh! -S

Ingredients:

  • 3 large tomatoes, sliced.
  • 3 cups lettuce leaves (about one head lettuce)
  • 2 pcs. sweet bell peppers (capsicum), julienned
  • a palm full of sweet corn kernels
  • a palm full sweet green peas
  • a palm full of canned artichokes (torn into bite size pieces)
  • one can tuna (in brine or oil)
  • 1/4 cup virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar or lemon
  • salt and pepper to taste.
