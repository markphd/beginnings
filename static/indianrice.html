INDIAN RICE ALA SABRINA



Here's how to make the dish

  1. About 5 to 6 cups cooked rice, in wide pan place olive oil
  2. Palm full of cashew or peanuts, a palm full of raisins, salt pepper to taste
  3. Sprinkle of cumin,
  4. Let fry all together
  5. Serve in bowl
  6. Put chopped parsely on top for the perfect garnish!!!

    7. This is a great thing to do with boring day old rice!!!

Simple fresh and delicious! Colors fantabulous too!
From my kitchen to yours with love, Mwahhh! -S

Ingredients:

  • about 5 to 6 cups cooked rice
  • olive oil
  • cashew or peanuts
  • raisins
  • cumin
