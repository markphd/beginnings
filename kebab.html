BEEF KEBAB BITES with DEL MONTE MMK Dipping Sauce



Here's how to make the dish

  1. Mix all together then add a whole egg
  2. Roll meat into balls
  3. In heated pan with olive oil place individual meat rolls
  4. Turn to all sides until all golden brown
  5. Stick into toothpicks and serve with a siding of yogurt and cucumber salad and a dipping sauce of del monte mmk tomato sauce.
  6. The smells of the cumin garlic onions and beef will be absolutely irresistable!!
  7. Enjoy!
Simple fresh and delicious! Colors fantabulous too!
From my kitchen to yours with love, Mwahhh! -S

Ingredients:

  • one cup ground beef
  • half a cup chopped onions
  • half a cup chopped garlic
  • 3 tablespoon del monte mmk tomato sauce
  • half a teaspoon cumin
  • a palm full of chopped parsley
